What is Dragonball Z? Well, it takes a while to explain, but the very short answer is that Dragonball Z is an anime (Japanese animated series) that (mostly) revolves the warrior Goku and his friends battling villians in epic fights that literally destroy planets.


Dragonball Z was hugely popular in Japan and became similarly popular in the United States in the late 1990s and early 2000s.
